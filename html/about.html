About SympTrack

SympTrack is an AI-powered medical assistance platform designed to help you quickly understand your symptoms, get preliminary health insights, and find nearby doctors or hospitals. Our mission is to make healthcare information more accessible, accurate, and user-friendly for everyone.

Our Mission

With features like symptom tracking, health prediction, medical report analysis, and location-based recommendations, SympTrack empowers you to take informed steps toward your health and well-being. We aim to bridge the gap between people and healthcare by providing a smart, reliable, and easy-to-use platform for early symptom assessment and medical guidance.
